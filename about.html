Hello, we are Sanjana, Noah, and Kisa, members of the team behind the Local Air Quality App. Our goal is to raise awareness about the critical issue of urban air quality and provide a user-friendly platform for monitoring and tracking air quality metrics.

Background

We are currently students at The Unviersity of Maryland, majoring in Information Science. Passionate about environmental issues, we are excited to contribute to creating a tool that empowers individuals to stay informed about the air they breathe.

Vision

Our vision is to make a positive impact on urban communities by providing a tool that not only educates individuals about air quality but also encourages collective efforts towards a healthier environment. We aim to engage citizens, environmentalists, health professionals, and government agencies in this crucial initiative.
